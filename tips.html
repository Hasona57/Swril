Gaming Tips & Tricks

Some gaming tips I learned that might help you get better at games. These worked for me so maybe they'll work for you too.

FPS Gaming Tips

  • Turn down mouse sensitivity for better aim
  • Keep crosshair at head level
  • Learn the maps
  • Listen for footsteps
  • Don't spray bullets everywhere

RPG Gaming Tips

  • Save your game a lot
  • Talk to NPCs for side quests
  • Clean out your inventory
  • Take your time with the story
  • Try different character builds

General Gaming Tips

  • Take breaks so your eyes don't hurt
  • Keep your setup clean
  • Update your graphics drivers
  • Join Discord servers to make friends
  • Don't rage quit when you lose

Gaming Setup Advice

Monitor

Get at least 144Hz if you play competitive games. The difference is huge once you try it. Size doesn't matter as much as refresh rate and response time.

Peripherals

A good mouse and keyboard make a big difference. Mechanical keyboards feel amazing and gaming mice have better sensors than regular ones.

Audio

Good headphones help you hear enemy footsteps and directional audio. You don't need expensive ones, just something with decent sound quality.
