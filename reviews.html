My Game Reviews

Here's what I think about some games I played. I just rate them on how much I liked them and if they're worth playing.

Cyberpunk 2077

Rating: 7/10

Finally played this game after hearing it got better. The story is pretty good and the city looks cool. Graphics are really nice on my PC.

Combat is fun but gets boring after a while. I like the hacking stuff and all the different upgrades you can get.

It's a decent game now that they fixed most bugs. Worth playing if you like sci-fi games but still has some glitches.

Elden Ring

Rating: 9/10

This game is really hard but amazing. I died so many times but kept playing because the world is beautiful. There's always something cool to find.

Boss fights are crazy hard. Some took me hours but winning feels so good. Combat is smooth once you learn it.

If you played Dark Souls you'll love this. If not, expect to die a lot but don't quit! It's worth it when you finally beat a boss.

Among Us

Rating: 8/10

This game was huge during covid and I can see why. It's simple but really fun with friends. Every game is different.

Being the impostor is scary but fun. You have to lie and sabotage stuff. Being crew is cool too trying to figure out who's lying.

Best with voice chat and friends. Playing with randoms online is weird sometimes. But with good people it's really funny.
